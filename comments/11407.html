Elongated cluster of lustrous cassiterite crystals fully crystallized front and rear. The crystals around the edges show translucency. Sensel originally purchased the specimen from Kosnar, labelled "Huari-Huari mine", in 1983 for $125. Ex. Dr. E. Sensel collection #M291. Overall size of mineral specimen: 7x3x2.5 cm. Size of individual crystals: 3-10 mm.
