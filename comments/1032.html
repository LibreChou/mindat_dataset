Collected ca. 1910, this is an excellent large specimen of yellow rhombic calcite crystals up to 20 mm with many 1 mm pyrite crystals coating the calcite. No damage. Ex. Gilman Stanton, Hugh Ford, R. Fitton #628. Overall size of mineral specimen: 9x8x4 cm. Size of individual crystals: 1-20 mm.
