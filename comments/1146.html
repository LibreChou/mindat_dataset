Single crystal of colorless topaz with steep chisel-shaped termination. A few chips in the termination are present due to the alluvial nature of this locality. The label indicates that it was obtained in trade from Gunnar Bjareby in 1965. Ex. A. Stevenson collection #238. Overall size of mineral specimen: 23x20x17 mm. Size of individual crystals: 23 mm.
