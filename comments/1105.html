Hemispherical crystal cluster of white pectolite with small elongated apophyllite crystals up to 2 mm long scattered on the surface. The apophyllite has uncommon green inclusions, quite bright color for this locality. Collected in 1963. Ex. A. Stevenson collection #31. Overall size of mineral specimen: 3x3x2 cm. Size of individual crystals: 1-15 mm.
