This superb specimen of purple creedite is from the early 1980's find in Santa Eulalia which has set the standard for this species. Complete without damage, the specimen features gemmy crystals to almost 1 cm. This specimen was once in the John Barlow collection and retains his typed, glued on collection number B137. Photo by Edward Rosenzweig. 8.9 x 4.0 x 3.8 cm.
