11.5x 7.5x 7 cm. A very rich matrix lapis, the largest crystal, shown in the close up is 1.8 cm across. There is also a slightly larger crystal, with a band of pyrite through it. Most lapis crystals are usually found as single crystals in marble, but this piece has a display face entirely covered with them.
