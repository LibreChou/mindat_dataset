Naturally occurring graphite cones (up to 20 microns tall) on the surfaces of graphite spheres etched from calcite. From a roadcut south of Gooderham, Ontario, Canada. John A. Jaszczak specimen and photo. See article by Jaszcak, Robinson, Dimovski and Gogotsi in CARBON 41 (2003) 2085.
